Welcome to PinballY!

PinballY is a "front end" menu system/game selector for virtual pinball cabinets, providing an arcade-style interface for scrolling through your virtual tables and selecting the one you'd like to play next. The idea is to make your pin cab feel more like an arcade machine and less like a Windows PC, by hiding the Windows desktop and letting you access most functions through the small set of buttons found on most pinball machines.
