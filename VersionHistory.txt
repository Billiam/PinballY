PinballY Update History

Added alternative high score display formats: alphanumeric 16-segment display style
(for 1980s solid-state machines with segmented displays); typewriter style (for EM machines).

Bug fix: the program could get stuck on startup searching for PinballX, if PinballX wasn't installed.
